### Trading Logic

**CFX-EvenOdd Bot Strategy**:

1. **Trade Type**: Even/Odd digit prediction
2. **Duration**: 1 tick
3. **Recovery**: Martingale on loss (default 2.0x multiplier)
4. **Reset**: Return to initial stake on win

**Signal Integration**:

-   **Hot Zone (overrepresented digits)** ‚Üí Expect continuation ‚Üí DIGITODD if digits 1,3,5,7,9 are hot
-   **Cold Zone (underrepresented digits)** ‚Üí Expect reversion ‚Üí DIGITEVEN if even digits are due
